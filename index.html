About Me

James Smith, British entrepreneur and software engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I co-founded Bugsnag in 2012, a popular real-time crash detection service for mobile and web applications, with Simon Maynard. Bugsnag was acquired by SmartBear Software in 2021.

From 2009 to 2012 I led the product team as CTO of mobile gaming platform Heyzap.

I've also built a number of popular open-source projects which you can find on GitHub.

Follow @loopj

Projects

Bugsnag - Co-founder and CEO (acquired by SmartBear Software in 2021)

Bugsnag is “mission control” for software stability.

Over 4,500 engineering teams such as Airbnb, Pandora, Yelp, Cisco, and Lyft trust Bugsnag as their software stability source of truth. We process over 1 billion crash reports every day from applications worldwide, with tens of thousands of software engineers using our dashboards every week.

Heyzap - CTO (acquired by Fyber in 2016)

Heyzap’s mobile apps had over 10 million users and millions of mobile installations and received funding Y Combinator, Union Square Ventures, Ashton Kutcher and others. Heyzap's profitable ads platform was used by leading games companies such as King (Candy Crush Adventure) and Zynga.

As first employee and CTO, I was responsible for making product direction decisions, prioritizing engineering work, managing the engineering team, scaling the Heyzap mobile applications and website to millions of users, hiring new talent, and developing new features for the mobile applications or web backend in Ruby, Java or JavaScript.
